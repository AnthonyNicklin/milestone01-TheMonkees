Meet the Band

The Monkees

We are an Amercian Rock and Pop band that has been playing since the 1960's. Having had a string of number #1 hits around the world we have never been more in demand. Our band started from humble beginings like most rock bands - in Davy Jones's Dad's garage.

The band's first gig was our high school prom were we played in a crowded school hall to our fellow students. After that memorable night we then went from club-to-club and bar-to-bar trying to persade bar and club managers to let us play.

Our first big hit was a Niel Diamond cover of "I'm a Believer" which unexceptially took off! Since then it has been a rollcoaster of a ride. Our 2019 tour starts in Feburay and will take us back state side for a number gigs that could be coming to you!

Artists

Davy Jones - Bass

A multi-talented artist who has acted and published autobiographies. After having success on the stage early on which included a Tony Award Nomination, Davy joined the band to become a Monkee.

Micky Dolenz - Drummer

Singer, musician, actor, writer, producer, and director, Micky has a wealth of showbizz and performance experience he brings to the band. Born and raised in LA, a life on stage was his natural calling card.

Michael Nesmith - Guitarist

A true Texan, Michael was in the US Air Force before joing the Monkees. He has a passion for folk music and is known for his creatively.

Peter Tork - Singer, Guitarist

Since a early age Peter had an intrest in music and learnt to play serval instruments. Before the Monkees he was a serious musician in New York.
